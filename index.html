Welcome to Room 216

Room 216 is the home of the Computer Club at CHS and the Programming, Adv. Programming, and APCS classes. This is our github where projects, ideas, and other information can be posted and accessed.

If you have something to commit to this site, please let a teacher or someone who has access know.

Programming Self-Study Resources

If you want to study a language and dive head first, pick one and use one of the free eBooks, interactive courses, or guides we hand selected.

HTML/CSS
